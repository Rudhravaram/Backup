The cause of loss as alleged by the insured representative is storm, cyclone and
heavy rainfall. The insured has submitted us news paper cuttings to support the
cause of loss.

SALVAGE:

We have considered a Fair & Reasonable salvage amount INR 10000/- towards
the loss.

EXCESS:

BACKGROUND & OCCURRENCE:

As per the Insureds representative on 18.06.2018 there was hevay Rain,storm with

and IT rack was damaged and simultaneously require repair work.
