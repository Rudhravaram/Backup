4.00 OCCURRENCE & CAUSE

    During our Visit, It was reported by Mr. Sudhir (Cluster In charge) that
    on dated 28.05.2019in the night 15 KVA DG Set on running condition
    Suddenly Said DG set was smocking out and caught fire. Site Gaurd was
    Notice fire in the DG set and immediately informed to Local Peoples. The
    Local Peoples were stated fire fighting activities with the help of fire
    extinguisher. After some time the fire was in control and doused the fire.
    Due to this sudden incident the DG Set completely burnt


    Thereafter, the insured’s representative reported the matter to
    Underwriters on dated 30.05.2019 and subsequently we were depute to
    Carryout survey and assessment of loss vide mail dated 31.05.2019.
