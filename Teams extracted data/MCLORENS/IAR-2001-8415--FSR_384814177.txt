4.00 OCCURRENCE & CAUSE

    During our Visit, It was reported by Mr. Bhupender (Site In Charge) that on
    dated 03.09.2019 in the evening at around 16:10 the heavy rain started
    suddenly which caused sudden inundation of water. The water level rose
    upto 5 to 7 feets thus giving no time and option to save the 15 KVA DG Set
    , IPDMU and 01 Set 300 Ah Battery Bank which was installed at Insured
     Site got affected

    Thereafter, the insured’s representative reported the matter to
    Underwriters on dated 05.09.2019 and subsequently we were depute to
    Carryout survey and assessment of loss vide mail dated 09.09.2019.
