2.0   INCIDENT


2.1   Pune region witnessed heavy rainfall in the end of September 2019.
      Various regions were affected and water had inundated in various
      places. On 24th September 2019 at around 10.00 pm – 10.30 pm, the
      compound wall portion of Geras Emerald City collapsed. Due to heavy
      rainfall in Baner area, water had inundated in the premises, which
      caused the wall to collapse. Insured had provided a CCTV footage and
      photographs in support of the incident. Even the parking area was
      inundated with water upto a height of 1-1.5 feet.
